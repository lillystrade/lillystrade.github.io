College

I am a Junior at the University of Wisconsin - Madison , studying Consumer Behavior and Marketplace Studies with certificates in Entrepreneurhsip and Digital Studies.

I have a passion for the everchanging world of retail. I am currently the Vice President of Internal Relations for The Student Retail Association at UW-Madison. Through this club, I have gained valuable knowledge and have had many unique experiences. These include:

  • Networking with retail professionals
  • Attending speaker events with professionals from big name retail companies
  • Traveling to different corporate headquarters to get a real sense of the industry
  • The opportunity to attend the National Retail Federation's Student Progam in New York City
  • Meet other students within my major that have similar interests
I am currently the Student Coordinator for the Leadership Certificate at the Center for Leadership & Involvement at UW-Madison. I started this position this past January and am currently loving it!

This past summer, I attended Kohl's Early Talent Summit. I heard from many different professionals about their experience working for Kohl's. I also learned about Kohl's mission, visions, and values. Additionally, I participated in a Case Competition challenging me and my team to come up with an idea that expands Kohl's customer base. This was a great opportunity for me to use the skills that I have learned in my first two years of college and build out an idea with my team.

My completion of the Talent Summit provided me with an early interview for an internship for the summer of 2024. This Summer, I will be an Assistant Merchant Intern at Kohl's Corporate Office in Menomonee Falls, WI. I am super excited to apply all of the skills and knowledge that I have learned here at UW, but also learn so much more as an intern! I am specifically excited to not only learn about the merchant position, but also shadow other departments to get a sense for my future.
