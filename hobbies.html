Reading

Reading is something that has come and gone throughout my years. I read a lot as a little kid, and fell out of this habit in middle school and high school. However, this past year, I have fell in love with it again. My goal for 2024 is to read 20 books! I keep track of all my progress and ratings on an app called "GoodReads." This is something that helps to calm my mind at the end of the night.
